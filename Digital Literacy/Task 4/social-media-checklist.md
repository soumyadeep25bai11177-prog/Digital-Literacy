## Social Media Responsibility Checklist

### Do's

* **Audit Privacy Settings:** Regularly check who can see your posts and tagged photos.
* **Network Professionally:** Use LinkedIn to connect with alumni and industry leaders.
* **Think Before You Post:** Consider if you'd be comfortable with a recruiter seeing the content.
* **Showcase Achievements:** Share projects, certifications, or volunteer milestones.
* **Verify Before Sharing:** Ensure news is from credible sources to avoid spreading misinformation.

### Don'ts

* **Don't Post During Class:** This signals a lack of engagement to potential academic or professional observers.
* **Don't Share Confidential Info:** Never post details about research, exams, or internship projects.
* **Don't Engage with Trolls:** Avoid public arguments that leave a permanent, negative digital footprint.
* **Don't Badmouth Others:** Avoid venting about professors, classmates, or employers.
* **Don't Overshare Personal Data:** Keep your specific location and private schedule off public forums.
